Best Natural beauty tips for normal skin:

 

• Clean your skin with a mild baby soap and water twice a day. If you like you can tone your skin with something mild, like rose water.
• Apply mild freshener after every cleansing with a mild freshener to keep your pores tight. This also helps in removing traces of cleanser clinging to skin.
• Always use an astringent with a low alcohol content.
• Avoid direct heat on the face, including from blow dryers.
• Use makeup products that contain sunscreen to avoid early drying, aging effects of direct sun heat.
• Apply a thin film of home-made moisturizer at night to keep your skin’s normal moisture.
• Use a mild, oil based moisturizer under your make up to retain normal moisture.
